ADAL Charter

Our guiding principles for democratizing academic research at CSPC

Mission Statement

ADAL AI's mission is to ensure that every CSPC student, faculty member, and researcher can:

  • Discover relevant research effortlessly through conversational search
  • Access complete thesis abstracts and metadata in seconds
  • Receive proper citations automatically to support academic integrity
  • Explore the breadth of CSPC research without technical barriers
01

Broadly Distributed Academic Benefits

We commit to using ADAL's capabilities exclusively for the benefit of the CSPC academic community, ensuring equitable access to research for all students and faculty, regardless of technical skill or department.

Our primary duty is to CSPC. We prioritize:

  • Student success: Helping students find relevant past research to inform their own work
  • Faculty efficiency: Reducing time spent manually searching for related studies
  • Research continuity: Preserving institutional knowledge across graduating cohorts
02

Accuracy and Source Transparency

We are committed to providing factually accurate answers grounded strictly in CSPC's thesis collection.

No hallucinations

ADAL will clearly state "I didn't find that information in my knowledge base" when data is unavailable

Source attribution

Every answer includes document sources and page numbers

APA compliance

Automatic citation formatting follows institutional standards

We will never generate fictional research or fabricate thesis content.

03

Privacy and Data Security

We protect user queries and chat history with institutional-grade security:

  • CSPC-only access: Email domain restrictions (@cspc.edu.ph, @my.cspc.edu.ph)
  • Encrypted storage: Supabase authentication and chat logs comply with academic data privacy standards
  • No external data sharing: User queries and research interests remain confidential within CSPC infrastructure
04

Continuous Improvement

We actively solicit feedback from the CSPC community to expand coverage and improve accuracy:

Roadmap transparency

Known limitations are clearly communicated

Semester updates

New thesis batches ingested after each academic year

Feature requests

Community-driven enhancements

05

Open Collaboration

We encourage collaboration with CSPC library staff, IT services, and academic departments:

  • Content curation: Librarians control which theses are indexed
  • Quality assurance: Faculty advisors can review retrieval accuracy
  • Technical transparency: System architecture fully documented
