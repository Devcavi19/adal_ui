About

AI-Powered Academic Librarian for CSPC

What We Do

ADAL AI is an AI-powered academic librarian built specifically for CSPC (Camarines Sur Polytechnic College) to modernize library research and discovery. Created and maintained by Team Virgo, ADAL transforms traditional keyword-based catalog searching into conversational, intelligent research assistance.

Conversational Search

Ask questions in natural language and get precise answers from CSPC's thesis and research collection

Smart Citations

Automatic APA-style citation generation for academic integrity

Exhaustive Retrieval

Request all research on a topic, not just the top results

Source-Linked Answers

Every response includes direct links to the original thesis documents

Current Coverage

Our knowledge base currently includes theses and research from:

BSM - Business Management
BSN - Nursing
CAS - College of Arts and Sciences
CCS - College of Computer Studies
CTHBM - Tourism, Hospitality & Business Management
